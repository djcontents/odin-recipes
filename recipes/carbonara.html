carbonara

Everyone loves Carbonara pasta. What’s the difference between this recipe?

Everywhere you can find carbonara pasta recipe with Bacon, Eggs, and Cheese. Here I do some changes do the usual. Our secret to this amazing Carbonara dish is that we add mushrooms and yogurt apart from ham. Does it taste nice! Of Course, it does. If you do not like the taste of Yoghurt or mushrooms or if you can’t find them in your kitchen right now, forget about them and go with the usual carbonara dish, skipping those. No harm to the taste!

Ingredients

  • 300 g Pasta – Any Kind
  • 50 g Mushrooms, Sliced Thinly
  • 1 Large Onions, Diced Finely
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 200 g Unsweetened Yogurt
  • 100 g Ham, Cut Into Small Pieces (Or Bacon)
  • 40 g Parmesan
  • 300 g Any Pasta Sauce (Not Mandatory)
  • Pepper, Crushed To Taste
  • Salt – To Your Taste

steps

  1. Boil the pasta in a large saucepan, make sure to add 1 teaspoon of butter/ oil to the saucepan before boiling the pasta. Do not over boil the pasta.
  2. Once done, remove the pot off the heat, drain pasta and keep aside.
  3. Mix together the yogurt, egg, cheese, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Add salt to your taste.
  5. Heat another separate frying pan or a wok and add the ham, saute until brown. Put aside it.
  6. In the same pan add the mushrooms and then add onions and garlic.
  7. Add the egg mixture to the same pan which has vegetables and stir well
  8. Remove just before they turn brown
  9. Mix with pasta, the heat from the pasta should thicken the sauce
  10. Do not put on high heat or the mixture will scramble the egg.
  11. Once done remove from heat.
  12. Add the ham and serve warm.
